Individual Goal

Start with the goal. Then add the amount you can begin with and the amount you may be able to contribute each month.

If checked, the planner applies your starting contribution toward the suggested infrastructure first. If the starting contribution is not enough, the remaining infrastructure amount is shown as something monthly contributions would need to cover before the broader goal plan continues.
