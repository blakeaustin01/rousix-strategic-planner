Interactive prototype

Choose a goal. Build a pathway. Start small or go bigger.

This planner helps you map a simple Rousix goal pathway. Enter a goal, a price, a starting contribution, and a monthly contribution. The tool shows what your plan looks like, what infrastructure tier may fit, and what your next step could be.

Important: This is an educational planning demo. It does not promise returns, profits, liquidity, mining output, financing approval, or final ownership. Start small, understand the process, and increase your commitment only when the plan makes sense to you.

Planning flow

  1. 01
    Start Planning Choose a goal such as a vehicle, home, equipment, or business asset.
  2. 02
    Enter Contribution Add your starting amount and monthly amount. Minimums can start low.
  3. 03
    Review Goal Gap See the difference between your goal and your direct contribution path.
  4. 04
    Compare Options Adjust price, timeline, amount, or infrastructure tier.
  5. 05
    Prepare Next Steps Copy, print, verify payment method, and discuss the plan.
