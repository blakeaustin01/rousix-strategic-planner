Simple onboarding

What happens next?

  1. Confirm basic information

    Begin with the information needed to create a planning record.

  2. Choose your goal

    Vehicle, home, equipment, truck, workstation, or business asset.

  3. Confirm your numbers

    Price, starting contribution, monthly contribution, and timeline.

  4. Review your roadmap

    Understand the infrastructure amount, plan base, remaining gap, and compare options.

  5. Decide your comfort level

    Start small if you want to build trust first. Increase your commitment only if the pathway makes sense to you.

Clear expectations

This planner does not provide financial, investment, tax, legal, credit, lending, mining, staking, or accounting advice.

It does not guarantee profit, returns, appreciation, liquidity, financing approval, payment approval, asset purchase, or ownership.

Hypothetical 5x, 10x, 15x, and 40x examples are planning examples only. They are not promises, forecasts, guarantees, or expected outcomes.

The purpose of this planner is to explain the pathway in simple language and help you decide what questions to ask before moving forward.
