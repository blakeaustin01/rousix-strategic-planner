Simple onboarding

What happens next?

  1. Input personal credentials

    Basic information needed to begin a real onboarding process.

  2. Choose your goal

    Vehicle, home, equipment, truck, workstation, or business asset.

  3. Confirm your numbers

    Price, starting contribution, monthly contribution, and timeline.

  4. Review your roadmap

    Understand the gap, compare options, and ask questions.

  5. Decide your comfort level

    Start small if you want to test the process. Increase your commitment only if the plan makes sense to you.
